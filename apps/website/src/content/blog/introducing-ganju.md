Today we're sharing **Ganju** — an open-source platform that connects AI
assistants to the things you actually work with: your documents, your tools, and
your apps.

## Why we built it

AI assistants are great at reasoning but blind to your context. Wiring them up to
your data usually means running servers, writing glue code, and worrying about
security. Ganju removes all of that: create a connection, add your content and
tools, and your AI is ready — in minutes, no code required.

## What you can do

- Search and answer from your own files.
- Let your AI use real tools: email, calendar, web search, Slack, and more.
- Turn the same setup into a bot on Telegram, Slack, WhatsApp, or Discord.

It's open source under Apache-2.0 — run it yourself, or let us host it for you.
