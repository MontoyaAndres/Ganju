Ganju connects your AI assistant to your own files, tools, and apps through a
single secure connection — no servers to run and no code to write.

## 1. Create a workspace

Sign up at [app.ganju.ai](https://app.ganju.ai) and create a project. This is
your private space where everything lives.

## 2. Add content and tools

- **Files** — upload documents or connect Google Drive / OneDrive.
- **Tools** — switch on email, calendar, web search, Slack, and more.
- **Prompts** — write instructions that shape how your AI behaves.

## 3. Connect your AI

Copy your connection URL and add it to Claude, ChatGPT, or Cursor. Your AI can
now search your content and use your tools.

## 4. Share it

Turn the same setup into a bot on Telegram, Slack, WhatsApp, or Discord so your
whole team can use it.
